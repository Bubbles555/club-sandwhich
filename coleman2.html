Coleman Coffee Roasters was established on a shoe string in 2010 by Jack Coleman on an industrial estate in Camberwell. The roastery has since moved to Spa Terminus in Bermondsey surrounded by other like-minded food specialists.

We work on a very characterful gas powered coffee roaster made in postwar Vienna in 1950. Otto Swadlo, the manufacturer and his Lurch-like assistant developed and built the machine in what was formerly a semi-automatic munitions factory.

We are mainly a wholesale operation supplying cafes and restaurants around central London, we also open to the public on Saturday mornings 8:30-2:00 at Spa Terminus serving coffees and beans to take home.

The small variety of coffees we stock are all of classical profiles from South America. If you have any enquiries, wholesale or otherwise feel free to get in touch.

Excitingly, we are going to open our own shop in Waterloo very soon!
